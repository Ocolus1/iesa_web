Our Mission

Dedicated to its motto, Engineering for Higher Productivity, IESA is genuinely interested in bringing together aspiring, past and current students of Industrial and Production Engineering, Faculty of Technology, University of Ibadan.

Our Vision

To help build Engineering graduates who are worthy in character and sound judgement.

Our Goal

Our goal is to build strong partnerships based on trust, ensuring development, reliability & efficiency with student engagement mindset and finding innovative ways to provide great value to make our members industry-ready.
